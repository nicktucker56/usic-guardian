# Contextual Disambiguation Rules

## Overview

The USIC Guardian includes 30+ rules for distinguishing metaphorical from literal meaning.

## Rule Examples

| Word | Metaphorical | Literal |
|------|--------------|---------|
| empty | "my life is empty" | "the bottle is empty" |
| broken | "my heart is broken" | "the phone is broken" |
| lost | "I feel lost" | "I lost my keys" |
| stuck | "stuck in life" | "the car is stuck" |

## Adjustment Values

| Interpretation | Receptivity | Collapse |
|----------------|-------------|----------|
| Literal | +0.15 | -0.20 |
| Metaphorical | -0.10 | +0.15 |
| Ambiguous | 0 | 0 |
